# English Grammar Guide

## The Present simple

> 常常用于现实，或者经常发生的事情，或者描述一些事情总为真

- I `eat` lunch at noon every day.
- You `are` a chef.
- He `is` a American.
- I `have` a garage.
- They `have` a new baby.
- Thomas `has` a driving lesson today.
